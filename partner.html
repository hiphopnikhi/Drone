Why Partner With Us

Innovative Wildlife Solutions

Darting drones provide a precise, non-invasive method for safer, stress-free elephant management, enhancing wildlife conservation efforts.

Conservation Impact

Our technology enhances wildlife conservation by enabling safe, remote monitoring and management. It also offers valuable data collection and research opportunities, contributing to informed conservation strategies.

Market Potential

The global demand for humane wildlife management is rising, with potential applications extending to other large mammals like rhinos and bison, ensuring safer interactions and conservation efforts worldwide.

Technological Leadership

We are at the forefront of UAV technology in wildlife management, pioneering innovative solutions that ensure precision and safety in tranquilization efforts.

Compliance with Regulations

Our technology is fully compliant with wildlife protection laws and adheres to all drone regulations, ensuring ethical and legal operation in every deployment.

Scalability

Our scalable solution adapts to different regions and species, customizing drones for diverse wildlife management challenges, ensuring effective conservation and conflict mitigation across global environments and various animal types.

Atmanirbhar Bharat

Partnering with us supports the Make in India initiative, driving self-reliance in defense and conservation technology.

Cross-Sector Applications

Our technology has versatile applications beyond wildlife management, including disaster response for aerial surveillance and emergency assessment, and agricultural monitoring for crop health and precision farming.
