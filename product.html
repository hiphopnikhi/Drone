Traditional Method


Traditional methods for tranquilizing elephants typically involve ground teams using dart guns to administer sedatives. This process requires getting dangerously close to the animals, often leading to stress, aggression, and the risk of injury for both elephants and humans.

The method is time-consuming and demands precise timing and accuracy, which is challenging under field conditions. In many cases, these factors contribute to a significant mortality rate, either from overdosing, underdosing, or physical injuries sustained during the chase.

The risks are compounded by the possibility of incomplete sedation, which can leave the animal partially conscious and highly agitated, posing further dangers.

Our Implementation

Presenting Darting drones,

Darting Drones offer a revolutionary approach to tranquilizing elephants, combining advanced UAV technology with precision targeting.

Unlike traditional methods, drones provide a non-invasive, remote solution that minimizes stress for the animal and reduces risks to humans. These drones can accurately administer tranquilizers from a safe distance, eliminating the need for close proximity, which often leads to dangerous encounters.

The process is faster, more efficient, and significantly reduces the chances of injury or incomplete sedation. By ensuring a humane and controlled tranquilization, darting drones represent a breakthrough in wildlife management, enhancing both safety and effectiveness.
